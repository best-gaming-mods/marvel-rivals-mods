## ❓ Common Questions

**Will these mods get me banned?**  
These are client-side visual mods. They don't give competitive advantages. However, any game modification carries some risk. Use at your own discretion.

**Do other players see my mods?**  
No. Client-side mods only affect your game. Other players see default skins and effects.

**How do I uninstall mods?**  
Use the mod manager's "Restore Original Files" button or delete the `~mods` folder.

**Do mods work after game updates?**  
Some mods may break after major updates. Mod manager will notify you of compatibility issues.

**Can I use multiple skin mods at once?**  
Yes. Mod manager handles multiple mods simultaneously.

**Where can I request new mods?**  
Check the mod manager's "Request Mod" feature or the modding community Discord.

**Do these mods affect performance?**  
Most mods have minimal performance impact. Some visual effect mods may improve FPS by removing effects.

**Can I use these mods in competitive mode?**  
Use at your own risk. Mods that alter gameplay visuals may be flagged. Skin-only mods are safer.
